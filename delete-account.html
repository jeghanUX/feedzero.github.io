Delete Your FeedZero Account

You can request the deletion of your FeedZero account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

Open the FeedZero app → go to Settings → tap Log out → then email us at feedzero.support@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

What data is deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your session history and focus stats
  • Your streak data
  • Your app preferences and allowlist

No data is retained after deletion is complete.

Contact

For any questions about account deletion, email us at feedzero.support@gmail.com
